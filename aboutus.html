About TSF CARES

We’ve learned the hard way how to fundraise the easy way.

We started TSF CARES in 2019, which is subsidiary of a company The Sparks Foundation, a non-profit organisation that is working to bring parity in education, making sure children have equal opportunity at success. Our experience showed us an alarming gap in the online donation software market, so we set out to create the simplest solution around.

More than 1,000 organizations and individuals with causes trust Donately to manage their online donations. Built with simplicity in mind, our features give you more time to raise money online and our software grows with you, so the possibilities are endless.

Ultimately, we believe time spent solving problems shouldn’t be wasted using clunky and expensive online donation software.
